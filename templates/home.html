Hot Hot Zero.

The aim of this report was to examine the opportunities for building a net-zero-energy residential high-rise building in a hot and humid climate, using a case study project. The project is a 10-story residential tower that will house approximately 380 occupants. It is located in a new planned mixed-use development to be located to the northwest of central Shenzhen, China.
