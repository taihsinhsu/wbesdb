Energy simulation.

The use of a physics-based software to predict the energy use of a building.There hundreds of software tools available for simulating the energy performance of buildings and building subsystems.

The Building Energy Software Tools (BEST) database lists over many of these programs, which range in capability from whole-building energy simulations to model input calibration to building energy auditing.
